What is BitLicense?

The BitLicense is the first comprehensive virtual currency regulation regime proposed in the United States and would have profoundly negative impact on the industry.

BitLicense Covers:

  • Receiving for transmission or transmitting through a third party
  • Custody of funds
  • Buying / selling as a customer business
  • Retail fiat to bitcoin conversion services
  • Controlling, administering or issuing virtual currencies

No Safe Harbor Provisions

The BitLicense has no onramp or “safe-harbor” provisions for startups. This will create high barrier to entry for new or pre-funded companies. Startups will likely need to exclude New York customers and otherwise avoid NY jurisdiction to focus on product development before subjecting themselves to BitLicense.
